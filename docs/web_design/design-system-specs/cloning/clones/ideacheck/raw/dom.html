FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the results?
IdeaCheck searches real-time market data, competitor info, and industry benchmarks, then uses AI to cross-reference and score your idea. It's not guessing, but it's also not a guarantee — it helps you make more informed decisions.
Where does the data come from?
Each analysis searches Google, competitor websites, App Store / Google Play, Crunchbase, tech media, and more, then cross-references with SaaS industry benchmarks.
What are the free plan limitations?
The free plan includes 1 AI analysis per month with 7-dimension scoring, competitor search, death predictions, and more. The paid plan unlocks 3 additional dimensions (Growth Strategy, Unit Economics, Timing) with unlimited analyses.
What's the difference between free and paid reports?
Free reports include 7 core dimensions. Paid reports add Growth Strategy, Unit Economics, and Timing Analysis for a total of 10 dimensions. All dimensions include data source citations.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, subscriptions are billed monthly and can be cancelled anytime. After cancellation, you can still use the service until the end of the billing period.
How long does an analysis take?
Usually 30 seconds to 1 minute. The AI needs to search multiple data sources and cross-analyze in real time, so it's a bit slower than a chatbot but delivers much deeper results.
