Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the action guides?
ShipYourIdea generates specific action plans based on your idea description and questionnaire answers. Each recommendation is tailored to your specific context, not template answers.
How is this different from asking ChatGPT?
ShipYourIdea uses a structured questionnaire to understand your situation (stage, resources, budget), then systematically generates action plans across 6 dimensions with timelines and tool recommendations.
What are the free plan limitations?
You can use 5 full AI action guide analyses per day, including all 6 sections, tool recommendations, Quick Wins, and more. Completely free.
How long does an analysis take?
Usually 30 seconds to 1 minute. The AI generates 6 sections of action plans tailored to your specific situation, each with short/mid/long-term planning.
Can I analyze multiple ideas?
Yes, up to 5 times per day. Each time can be a different idea.
Is my data saved?
Your analysis reports are saved in your account for future reference. We don't share or sell your data.
